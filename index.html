About Me

With over 9 years of professional tattooing experience, Sina creates deeply personal, custom tattoo designs that are as unique as each client. Based inside Mr Snips Barbers in the Badger Hill area of York, the private studio setting ensures a relaxed, one-on-one experience from first consultation through to aftercare.

Sina's approach is built on collaboration — every design begins with your idea, your story, and your vision. Whether you're after something bold and graphic or delicate and intricate, the goal is always to bring your concept to life in a style that suits your skin and personality.

Tattoo Styles

Specialising in a range of styles to suit every client:

  • Blackwork — Bold, graphic designs using solid black ink. Ideal for striking geometric patterns, illustrative work, and cover-ups.
  • Fine Line — Precise, delicate linework perfect for minimalist designs, detailed portraits, and subtle, elegant tattoos.
  • Watercolour — Soft, fluid colour washes that mimic the look of watercolour painting, bringing a painterly, artistic feel to your tattoo.
  • Old School & Traditional — Classic bold outlines, iconic imagery and timeless designs rooted in the rich heritage of tattooing.
  • Geometric — Clean, mathematical shapes and sacred geometry, from simple linework to complex mandala-inspired compositions.
  • Abstract — Expressive, freeform designs that push beyond conventional styles into something truly one-of-a-kind.
